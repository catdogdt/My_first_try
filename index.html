Introduction

STEM stands for Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics. It's an educational approach that integrates these subjects to teach critical thinking, problem-solving, and innovation. STEM focuses on real-world applications and hands-on learning to prepare students for future careers in science and technology.
